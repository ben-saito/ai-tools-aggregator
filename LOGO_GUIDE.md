## üé® Option 1: Canva (Free, 5 minutes)

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### Step 1: Access Canva
1. Go to https://canva.com
2. Sign up for free account (or login)
3. Search for "Logo" templates

#### Step 2: Find Template
1. In search bar, type: "AI logo" or "Tech logo" or "Robot logo"
2. Browse templates
3. Look for:
   - Minimalist design
   - Blue color schemes
   - Tech/AI themes
   - Simple geometric shapes

#### Step 3: Customize
1. Click on template to edit
2. **Change text** to: "AI Tools" or "ATA" or "AT"
3. **Change colors**:
   - Primary: #2563EB (Blue)
   - Secondary: #10B981 (Green)
   - Background: White (#FFFFFF)
4. Remove unnecessary elements
5. Keep it simple and clean

#### Step 4: Resize
1. Click "Resize" button
2. Set custom size: 512 x 512 px
3. Click "Copy & resize"

#### Step 5: Download
1. Click "Share" ‚Üí "Download"
2. Format: PNG
3. Check "Transparent background" (if available)
4. Download

#### Step 6: Create Additional Sizes
Repeat for:
- Twitter header: 1500 x 500 px
- Favicon: 192 x 192 px

**Company Name**: AI Tools Aggregator

**Tagline**: Discover the best AI tools for 2026

**Industry**: Technology / Software / AI

**Target Audience**:

- AI enthusiasts

- Logo (transparent background)
- Sizes: 512x512px, 1024x1024px, vector (SVG/AI)

- Product Hunt logo (simple, iconic)
