Content creators face increasing demands for high-quality output across multiple formats. AI tools in 2026 offer powerful assistance for writing, video, image, and music creation. This guide highlights the best AI tools for each content type.

## Writing & Copywriting

### 1. ChatGPT Plus (OpenAI)
- Blog posts, scripts, social media content
- Pricing: $20/month

### 2. Jasper AI
- Marketing copy, long-form content
- Pricing: $49/month

### 3. Grammarly
- Grammar checking, tone adjustment
- Pricing: $12/month (Premium)

- Video editing with transcription
- Pricing: $12/month (Creator)

### 3. CapCut Pro
- AI-powered video editing
- Pricing: $9.99/month
