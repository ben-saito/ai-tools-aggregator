## ğŸ¯ Key Metrics & KPIs

### North Star Metric: Daily Active Users (DAU)

**Why DAU**:
- Reflects actual usage, not just visitors
- Correlates with revenue potential
- Measures product-market fit
- Easier to scale than user count

### Supporting Metrics

#### Acquisition
- **Traffic**: Unique visitors/month
- **Sources**: Organic, referral, direct, social, paid
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitor â†’ Registered user
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Marketing spend / new users

#### Engagement
- **DAU/MAU Ratio**: Stickiness (target: 20%+)
- **Session Duration**: Avg time on site (target: 3+ min)
- **Pages per Session**: Engagement depth (target: 3+)
- **Return Rate**: Users returning within 7 days (target: 40%+)

#### Monetization
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**
- **Churn Rate**: Users/revenue lost per month (target: <5%)
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Revenue per user over lifetime

#### Retention
- **Day 1 Retention**: Users returning next day
- **Day 7 Retention**: Users returning after week
- **Day 30 Retention**: Users returning after month
- **Cohort Analysis**: Retention by signup date
