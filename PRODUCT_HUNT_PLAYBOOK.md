## ğŸ”¥ Growth Hacks for Launch Day

### 1. Time Zone Advantage
- Launch at 12:01 AM PST
- Get early upvotes from Asia/Europe (day time there)
- By the time US wakes up, you have momentum

### 2. Support Group
- Create WhatsApp/Telegram group with 20-30 supporters
- Share product at 12:01 AM PST
- Ask for upvote + comment
- Coordinate response timing (not all at once)

### 3. Cross-Promotion
- Partner with other makers launching same day
- Support each other's launches
- Share audiences

### 4. Maker Community
- Post in Indie Hackers: "We're live on PH!"
- Share in relevant subreddits (r/SideProject, r/Entrepreneur)
- Post in AI communities
- Slack/Discord channels for makers

### 5. Email Blast
- Send to newsletter subscribers (if any)
- Personal email to friends/supporters
- Don't ask for upvotes directly (against rules)
- Say: "We're live on PH, would love your feedback"

### 6. Influencer Leverage
- DM 5-10 micro-influencers (5k-50k followers)
- "We're live on PH, would love your thoughts"
- If they upvote/share â†’ amplification
